## Agri Soil.AI Soil Health Intelligence System
Agri soil is an AI solution designed for smallholder farmers to analyze soil composition and optimize fertilizer recommendation.
This innovation system addresses the critical need for data-driven agriculture practices among smallscale farmers who currently lack    access to soil composition knowledge.

### Problem Statement and Solution
Smallholder farmers face siginficant challenges in optimizing their agricultural practices due to lack of soil composition knowledge.
They typically apply uniform fertilizers without understanding soil-specific requirements, resulting in substantial waste, increased costs, and suboptimal yields.
